About Eaglercraft & EaglercraftX

Eaglercraft and EaglercraftX is a Minecraft 1.5.2 and 1.8.8 Javascript port, respectively. You can play them on any modern web browser, courtesy of LAX1DUDE and ayunami2000. Singleplayer mode is included, but you have to wait a couple minutes for everything to load. Singleplayer isn't currently available on 1.8.8.
